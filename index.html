Welcome!

Hiya! This site is a place for me to put my projects and show off things i've made over the past 4 years. Enjoy my technical meanderings and all that.

Projects

bStream

bStream is a little thing I threw together while developing some of my game modding tools to make handling reading and writing binary files easier in C++.

Chippy

An experiment in emulation made to emulate the Chip8 system. While its a tad buggy, it works and was a fun way of learning how to write an emulator.

Luigi's Mansion Tools

A repository containing a few of the tools I wrote for modding the 2001 GameCube game Luigi's Mansion. They work with varying degrees of success and some of them are unfinished.
