JMP 3D Rewritten

JMP 3D Rewritten is the python rewrite of the experimental JMP 3D, an editor for the original Luigi's Mansion built off of Demolisher, a bin veiwing tool by arookas that you can find here. While its currently not yet finished, it can already edit jmp files, and even though the experimental c# version included bin model loading JMP3D will not feature that until later in its development life cycle.
